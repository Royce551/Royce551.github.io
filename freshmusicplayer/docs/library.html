The library represents your music collection.

Add stuff

There's 2 ways to add stuff to the library

  • Drag and Drop

    You can drag and drop a file or folder to import it. Be careful not to drop it into the controls box; that'll play it without importing!

  • Import Page

    The Import page offers a variety of ways to add tracks to your library.

Play stuff

The Tracks, Artists, Albums, and Playlists pages provide a variety of ways to view your library.

In the Artists, Albums, and Playlists pages, the pane on the left selects the artist, album, or playlist you want. You can also drag the edge to make it larger, if you need to.

The bar on the bottom shows how many tracks are displayed, followed by how long all of them are. Press the Enqueue All button to add all visible tracks to the queue, and press the Play All button to play all visible tracks.

Search your library

Click the search icon in the Navbar to bring up the search auxiliary pane. Then, start typing an artist, title, or album.
