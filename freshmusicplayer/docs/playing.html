The Queue

The queue is the list of tracks that FMP will play. You can view it by clicking the Queue icon in the Navbar.

From here, you can add or remove tracks from the queue. Double click a entry or click its play button to skip to it. Click its delete button to remove it from the queue.

Playing Music

You can play a track from the library by double clicking it, or clicking its Play button.

To open a track that isn't in the library, drag it into the Controls Box, or click Open in the Overflow Menu.
