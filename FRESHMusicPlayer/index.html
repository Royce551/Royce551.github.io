Don't spend several minutes waiting on your audio player!

FRESHMusicPlayer is designed from the ground up to start up and get you to your favorite tracks quickly.

FRESHMusicPlayer has lots of cool features, like playlists, support for embedded lyrics, a tag editor, a miniplayer, and more!

It also integrates with System Transport Controls, so you can control your music on the lock screen or with your keyboard.
