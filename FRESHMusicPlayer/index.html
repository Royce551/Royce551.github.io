Features

Speedy

Starts up, shows tracks, and updates quickly

Library

Organizes your music collection into tracks, artists, and albums, with quick search and playlist support

Track Information

Lets you view audio file's embedded tags, including lyrics (embedded or LRC files), and edit them using the built in tag editor

Integrations

Integrates with System Transport Controls on Windows 10 so that you can control your music with your keyboard or on the lockscreen, and with Discord, if you'd like to share what you're listening to. Plus, you can add song metadata from popular databases like Discogs and MusicBrainz

Mini Player

Fits snugly in your taskbar (or anywhere else!) and stays out of your way
